Romance

Toradora! (2008) A classic romance anime featuring Ryuuji Takasu, a kind-hearted but intimidating-looking guy, and Taiga Aisaka, a small but fiery girl. They team up to help each other get closer to their respective crushes but end up developing feelings for each other instead.
Horimiya (2021) A wholesome rom-com about Kyoko Hori, a popular and responsible girl, and Izumi Miyamura, a quiet and introverted guy who has a totally different side outside school. Their relationship blossoms as they discover each other's true selves.
Kaguya-sama: Love is War (2019) A high-IQ rom-com where Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, two top students at a prestigious academy, are in love but refuse to confess first. They engage in hilarious mind games to make the other confess first, leading to a battle of wits filled with comedy and romance.
Charlotte (2015) A supernatural drama where certain teenagers develop special abilities. Yuu Otosaka, who can temporarily possess others, gets involved with Nao Tomori and a special academy for ability users. The story takes a deep emotional turn as Yuu faces major sacrifices.
Your Name (2016) A breathtaking romance movie about Mitsuha Miyamizu, a countryside girl, and Taki Tachibana, a Tokyo boy, who mysteriously swap bodies. As they try to navigate each other's lives, they uncover a heart-wrenching connection that transcends time and fate.
Darling in the Franxx (2018) A mecha anime with romance and psychological themes. In a dystopian world, children are raised to pilot giant robots (Franxx) to fight mysterious creatures. Hiro, a struggling pilot, meets Zero Two, a mysterious girl with horns. Their relationship unfolds as they uncover the dark truths behind their world.
Yahari Ore no Seishun Love Comedy wa Machigatteiru. (My Teen Romantic Comedy SNAFU) follows Hikigaya Hachiman, a cynical and antisocial high school student forced to join the Service Club. There, he works alongside the sharp-tongued Yukino Yukinoshita and the cheerful Yui Yuigahama, helping others with their problems.
